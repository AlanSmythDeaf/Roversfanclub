Fan Club

Welcome to the Shamrock Rovers fanclub website! The fanclub was established in 2020 to support the Shamrock Rovers football club. We are now a group of pfans who love Rovers and want to share our enthusiasm with others. Our Fanclub is dedicated to providing the latest news, updates, and information about the team and fans. Whether you’re a die-hard fan or just getting into the sport, we have something for everyone. Join us in cheering on the Shamrock Rovers and let’s show our support for this amazing team and fans!

Shamrock Rovers

Founded in Ringsend, Dublin in 1899, it was said that for a short period the club also operated a team under the name of St. Patrick’s in the unaffiliated Sunday League. It has not been possible to garner any further information on this though. Rovers won the League title at our first attempt in the 1922–23 season and established themselves as Republic of Ireland most successful club by 1949, winning 44 major trophies. We’ve been based in Tallaght since 2009. Shamrock Rovers is a member-owned club and is run by a board of directors elected by, and from, its membership. The structure is members based with director involvement in all decisions. This is very important to the club and is central to the philosophy of the club. Shamrock Rovers is the most successful club in the Republic of Ireland. With a record 21 League of Ireland titles, including four in a row from 1984-1987, and again from 2020-2023, as well as a record 25 FAI Cups, Rovers have also supplied more players to the Republic of Ireland national football team than any other club.
